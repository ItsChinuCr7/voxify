Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about how Voxify works and how it fits into public and enterprise environments.

How are complaints routed to departments?

Complaints are automatically routed based on category, location, and predefined department rules, ensuring faster and accurate assignment.

Can citizens track the status of their complaints?

Yes. Every complaint and outage has a real‑time status that can be viewed by citizens, promoting transparency throughout the resolution process.

How does Voxify handle service outages?

Outages can be declared instantly and updated in real time. Public status updates keep users informed while internal notes remain restricted.

Does Voxify support SLA tracking and escalations?

Voxify tracks SLA timelines automatically and escalates unresolved issues based on priority and time rules, ensuring accountability.

Can Voxify be customized for different departments?

Yes. Each department can have its own workflows, categories, permissions, and escalation policies.

Is Voxify secure for government and enterprise use?

Voxify includes role‑based access control, audit logs, and data segregation to meet enterprise and government security requirements.
